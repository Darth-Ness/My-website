Like a lot of people, I use addons on my (Firefox) browser. In this post, I'm going to describe my addons, and explain why I use them.
Panorama Tab Groups:
Panorama Tab Groups is a tab managing addon that instead of putting
your tabs in an list, it puts your tabs in grid that takes up the whole screen.
Tridactyl:
This is an addon that is best summed up with a quote "Vim, but in your browser" -Tridactyl
It allows you to control just about everything in Firefox with the keyboard.
UBlock Origin: This one probably needs no intro, its an ad blocker. With no noticeable effect on system
resources, as well as being open source, it easily the best browser based add blocker.
